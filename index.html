About Me

Full Stack Developer specializing in programming, with skills in JavaScript, Bootstrap, SQL, and Python.

Experienced 3D Artist/Designer in the fast-paced Tradeshow industry working with digital design tools such as Adobe CS and 3DS Max. Works well when collaborating with others or independently.

Although I have a background in computer 3D graphics, I realized that my true passion is programming. I enjoy building and problem solving with programming more than designing.

I am currently taking an intense Full Stack Coding Boot Camp at Northwestern, acquiring real-world skills in HTML/CSS, JavaScript, Python, Node, SQL, and ORM. I recently earned a Udacity Full Stack Web Developer Nanodegree and developed an Item Catalog Web Application with CRUD functionality.

My bootcamp experience, being self-reliant, having worked in a fast paced environment, and my passion for programming will position me well to become a strong programmer.

While taking a break from coding my family and I like to travel to at least one National Park every year. We bring a National Parks Passport where we stamp the name of the park we are visiting. Sometimes we hike or take a tour (so someone else can drive and I can enjoy the ride.) This past summer we visited Yosemite, Sequoia and King's Canyon National Parks in California.
